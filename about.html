Why Urban Pumpkin

We are working hard to create lovely apartments that make you feel at home, but with the hotel amenities you love so much.

We want you to have the freedom and privacy you get in your home but with the extras that make you not want to ours .

Imagine your dream apartment, our vision is to create that for you.

We're going somewhere

Today, we are starting out of Lagos Nigeria with a few apartments. But our end game is to change the face of hospitality across Africa and we are doing this through standardisation and technology.

We want you to find a pumpkin in every African country you visit. Though each individual pumpkin is unique, e maintain the same unadulterated standard and unifying elements across all our apartments.

It's just like having your home in your backpack, thats the urban pumpkin feeling.

Harnessing technology

From check-in to check-out, your journey has minimal human interaction except you expressly request for it. We've deployed a keyless entry system making your bookings as seamless as can be. When you book and pay for a pumpkin apartment, you automatically receive an access code which grants you access to the apartments for the number of days you have booked.

If you require additional services such as cleaning, you're one click away from booking a cleaning slot from the easy to use concierge section of our website. Once booked, a cleaning representative will be with you at your chosen time.

Our chat bot function infused with the latest artificial intelligence technology is also available to answer all your questions. However, for times when you would rather interact with a human representative, our concierge functions connects you directly to a member of our team.

Our Leadership

Amanda founded Urban Pumpkin in April 2022 when she noticed a gap in the short term rental (STR) market in Nigeria. The industry was lacking standardisation with regards to STR offerings and Amanda embarked on a journey to fix this.

Before Urban Pumpkin, Amanda was Managing Director at Xenia Lifestyle, an indigenous hospitality management company focused on curating hospitality services.

Amanda holds two MSc degrees in Disruptive Innovation and International Marketing, both from Hult International Business School, where she graduated with dual distinctions and a BSc. in Finance from the University of Lagos.
